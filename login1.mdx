## LOGIN PROCESS

Users have the flexibility to sign up with their email or by social login liek google,discord and x to name a few, which seamlessly generates a wallet linked to their account, streamlining the process.
Alternatively, for those who prefer the web3 route, there's still the option to connect through a standard wallets method.

This dual approach caters newcomers and exchanges users, as the flow will match to what they're used to without affecting seasoned web3 users
